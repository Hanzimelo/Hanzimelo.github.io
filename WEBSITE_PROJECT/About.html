Cafe Thea Restaurant

Cafe Thea is a food catering service based in Taguig that provides packed meals, hosted events, and catering services. The company then relocated its main branch to Taguig, where it would remain until today.

About Us

About Us

Welcome To Cafe Thea Retaurant

Our company always seeks to provide the best possible experience to ensure that your special day is the best that it can be!

Cafe Thea began as a humble family restaurant in Manila, run by five sisters who wished to share their food with the entire country. This restaurant served locales and tourists alike, providing home-cooked meals that were both nutritious and delicious. But in 2017, the sisters received an opportunity to expand. After several years of remaining as a small restaurant, the business finally grew to become a catering service that had more reach across the entirety of Manila.
